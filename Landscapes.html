Yellowstone National Park
The funny story behind this picture is I waited 45 minutes to take the picture. Everytime I thought I had a shot, someone would walk through the picture or was nearby. In the end, it was worth the wait. It is one of my favorite pictures.

Yellowstone Road
The funny story behind this pic is I was driving to another spot in the park and realized no one was coming down the road and there was this quick window to capture this photo. There was a dead silence for the 10 minutes I was out of the car and trying to find the perfect spot in the middle of the road. I have to say, this was the most breathtaking spot in my experience thus far. The air was clean, crisp and it felt very remote.

The remaining pictures are from another visit to Yosemite National Park which is another one of my favorite spots. The other picture is from one my many drives along the Pacific Coast Highway.
